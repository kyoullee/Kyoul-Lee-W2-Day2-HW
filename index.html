Introducing California's Greatest Cliffs

What is so unique about California cliffs?

California's cliffs are unique in the United States, offering unparalleled beauty. It is the only state with cliffs that front all possible environments: sea, river, mountains, and canyons.

There are four such unique cliffs: West Cliff, North Cliff, East Cliff, and South Cliff. Each offers breathtaking views, recreational areas, camping sites, wildlife observation spots, and overnight cabins.

Where are these cliffs?

Located within a 100-mile radius from Irvine, California, these cliffs are easily accessible by road. The closest to Irvine, West Cliff, is also the most visited and popular.
